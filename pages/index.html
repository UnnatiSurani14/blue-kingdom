Our Boats

We have two unique boats to fit your group's needs! Sounder is our open-air, Zodiac-style vessel. Wake is our semi-enclosed runabout-style vessel. Both boats are fast and fun for reaching a wide variety of locations, which gives us one of the highest whale sighting rates in the Pacific Northwest! Both boats are equipped with onboard restrooms.
